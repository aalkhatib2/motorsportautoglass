Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Motorsport Autoglass ("we," "our," or "us") provides mobile windshield and auto glass replacement and repair services in Florida and Arizona. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you provide when you use our website, request a quote, or book a service appointment.

Information We Collect

When you request a quote or book a service through our website, we may collect:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Your vehicle's year, make, and model
  • Details about the service requested (e.g., windshield, side window, or rear glass; repair or replacement)
  • Insurance provider information, if applicable
  • Your service address and preferred appointment date/time

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Schedule and fulfill your requested glass repair or replacement service
  • Contact you regarding your appointment, quote, or service status
  • File and process insurance claims on your behalf, where applicable
  • Improve our services and website

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. Mobile opt-in data and phone numbers collected through our website or booking system are not shared with any third party for their own marketing use.

Text Messages (SMS)

If you provide your phone number, Motorsport Autoglass or its staff may send you text messages related to your service request, appointment confirmation, or follow-up communication regarding your booking. Message frequency varies depending on your interaction with us (typically 1–5 messages per service request). Message and data rates may apply. You may reply STOP at any time to opt out of text communications, or reply HELP for assistance.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect the personal information you provide from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party service providers (such as scheduling, communication, or payment processing tools) to help operate our business. These providers are only given access to the information necessary to perform their function and are not authorized to use your information for their own marketing purposes.

Your Choices

You may contact us at any time to ask what information we have on file, request corrections, or request deletion of your information, subject to any recordkeeping obligations related to insurance claims or completed services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
